About

Cinematic orchestral music for stories that need scale, drama, and heart.

Solunea is the artist name of Junho Shin, a Korean composer specializing in cinematic and epic orchestral music for games, films, and visual media. His work focuses on powerful melodies, dramatic orchestration, and immersive soundscapes that support storytelling and build emotional worlds.

With a strong sense for melody, he creates music that feels adventurous, emotional, and larger than life. Alongside composition, he enjoys playing guitar and piano, which continue to inspire his musical ideas and creative voice.

Tailored Scoring Custom-built music designed to fit the emotion, pacing, and atmosphere of each scene.
Narrative Driven Music shaped to support storytelling, character, and the emotional flow of visual media.
High-Fidelity Clean, polished audio crafted with clarity, depth, and cinematic impact.
